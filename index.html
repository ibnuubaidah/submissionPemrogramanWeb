Home

Homeschooling is a family that chooses to take responsibility for their children's education and educate their children on a home basis. In homeschooling, parents are fully responsible for the children's education process; while in regular schools that responsibility is delegated to teachers and the school system.

Supported by a solid management team

Consists of psychologists, professionals who are experts in their fields as well as qualified and dedicated teachers in the world of education and have a long experience

Interactive method and not boring

Effective for developing children's skills, intelligence and talents

Legal Certificate

The exams and certificates provided are not only local but national and international and are recognized by the Ministry of National Education.

Process Oriented and Material Understanding

At Eleven Homeschooling it is taught not to chase numbers in the short term in a short way but to achieve competent values that will be useful in the medium and long term.

Learning Type

Based on the type of learning we provide two classes, that is :

Online Class

With this online class, students will study at home online with supporting applications. And a mentor or teacher will teach students remotely

Offline Class

With this online class, students will study at home. And a mentor or teacher will teach students face to face. Can be at the student's house, or in the place that we have provided
